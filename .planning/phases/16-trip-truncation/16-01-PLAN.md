<objective>
Add configurable trip duration truncation to MOR estimator with default 30-minute threshold.

Purpose: Prevent unstable variance estimates from very short incomplete trips by filtering trips below minimum duration threshold before MOR estimation. Following Hoenig et al. (1997) recommendations, short trips (<30 min) can produce extreme catch/effort ratios that dominate variance.

Output: `truncate_at` parameter in `estimate_cpue()` defaulting to 0.5 hours, filtering trips before MOR estimation, variance computed on truncated sample only.
</objective>
