## Decisions Made

- Used `base::merge()` with no `by` argument for cross-join instead of `dplyr::cross_join()` to avoid requiring dplyr >= 1.1.0 (as stated in the plan)
- Row ordering after merge sorts by date, then period_id (if present), then window_id for intuitive field dispatch order
- Validation checks `is.data.frame()` and required column names before merging; missing columns are named in the error message
